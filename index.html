SpaceBlimp

SpaceBlimp is an educational exploratory stratospheric balloon project that launches payloads into near-space.

We are planning a practice launch in late January or early February with a primary launch in April 2018 participating in the Global Space Balloon Challenge (GSBC).

SpaceBlimp 7 is a collaboration between HacDC (Washington, DC), Rockville Makerspace (Rockville, MD), and Unallocated Space (Severn, MD). Our mission is to create a curriculum that will successfully launch payloads while teaching students how to work with several groups and instructors together. It is aimed to expose middle and high school students to educational and technical resources outside of a traditional school environment as well as giving all participants the opportunity to share skills and interests to work together, learning from one another to achieve shared goals.

Local area hackers, space enthusiasts, and community members have come together to share and learn new skills in the design, construction, and launch of the project.
